OUR STORY

AND VISION

Welcome to Rainy Days

The embodiment of a journey that echoes the resilience of the human spirit
and the majesty of nature. Our tale begins with a man and his wife who traded
their urban comfort for the windswept landscapes of western Norway.
Leaving behind lucrative careers, they sought solace in the embrace of
relentless rain and ever-changing skies.

Years of navigating the elements sculpted their perspective, birthing a revelation of functional and
fashionable adventure gear shouldn't be a luxury but a right for all. Thus, Rainy Days was conceived,
fueled by their ardor for both thrilling escapades and equitable companionship.

IN THE SYMPHONY OF LIFE RAINY DAYS COMPOSE THE MOST BEAUTIFUL NOTES

This credo underpins our mission. Our gear is more than fabric;
it's the means to defy the tempests, both literal and metaphorical.
Rainy Days champions the notion that life's challenges can be
transformed into invigorating walks in the park, even when
thunderclouds gather.
From meticulously engineered jackets that laugh in the face of rain
to steadfast boots that conquer rugged terrains,
every creation is an ode to the adventurous spirit within us all.
Step into Rainy Days, where weathering the storm is a privilege,
and let us outfit you for life's most daring exploits,because after all,
the most vibrant rainbows follow the rain.
